      Welcome to Exeter Pubs

      A small, opinionated guide to 192 pubs, bars and clubs across Greater Exeter and the surrounding villages. Curated by hand — no ads, no accounts, no tracking.

      What you can do

      • 📍 Find pubs near you — share your location and they sort by walking distance.
      • 🔍 Search & filter — by name, area (Topsham, Exmouth, Crediton…), or tag (Real Ale, Garden, Dog Friendly…).
      • 📓 Keep your own notes — date a visit, star a pub, write what you thought. Tap "+ Add my notes" on any card.
      • 🍻 Plan a pub crawl — pick stops, drag to reorder, save it, or share a link with a friend.

      A few things to know

      • Your notes and saved crawls live in your browser only. Use Settings ⚙ to export them as a backup.
      • Pub data comes from OpenStreetMap, then tagged and cleaned by hand. Tags are best-guess and always improving.
      • Shared crawl links work without an account — recipients see your stops on their own copy of the app.

      A note from the maker

      This is a passion project. I built it because I wanted a calmer alternative to the big review sites — somewhere to keep my own pub diary and plan good nights out without all the noise.

      If you find something wrong, a pub that's closed, or you've got a pub I've missed — drop me a line. — Max
